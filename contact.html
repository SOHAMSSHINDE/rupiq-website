Frequently Asked Questions
Is Rupiq free to use?
Yes! Rupiq is completely free to download and use. The app is supported by non-intrusive ads to keep it free for everyone.
Which calculators are available?
Rupiq offers EMI (Loan Planner), SIP, FD (Fixed Deposit), and RD (Recurring Deposit) calculators — each with Compare and Goal Planning modes.
Does the app work offline?
Yes! All calculators work without an internet connection. Your data never leaves your device.
Is Rupiq available on iOS?
Currently Rupiq is available on Android only. iOS support may be added in the future based on demand.
How do I report incorrect calculations?
Please email report.bug@rupiq with the calculation type and the values you entered. We'll investigate and push a fix quickly.
Are my financial inputs stored anywhere?
No. All calculations happen locally on your device. We never store, collect or share any data you enter into the calculators.
